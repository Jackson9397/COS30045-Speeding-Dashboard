Road Safety Enforcement — Speeding

Interactive visual analytics of Australian speeding enforcement using BITRE open data (2008–2024). Explore trends, compare jurisdictions, and see where enforcement is most intense.

Open Dashboard Meet the Team

What’s inside

  • Bar Chart (Q1): Identifies the jurisdiction with the highest total fines across all years.
  • Line Chart (Q2): Shows long-term trends in camera-issued vs police-issued speeding fines from 2008 to 2024.
  • Pie Chart (Q3): Highlights which age group received the most fines in 2024.
  • Area Chart (Q4): Shows average fine amounts by state for 2024, with interactive hover guidelines.
  • Horizontal Bar Chart (Q5): Compares 0–16 age-group speeding fines across all states in 2024.

Data Source

BITRE Road Safety Enforcement Data (2008–2024). Includes speeding infringements captured via police-issued and camera-issued methods.

Source: Department of Infrastructure / BITRE — Road Safety Enforcement Data .
