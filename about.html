Methodology

  • Derived from BITRE Road Safety Enforcement data (2008–2024), using custom-processed CSV files for each analytical question.
  • Five question-specific datasets were prepared (Q1–Q5), each containing only the fields required for its visualisation.
  • Data cleaning and aggregation performed externally (grouping by year, jurisdiction, age group, and offence type).
  • D3.js used to generate five chart types: bar, column, pie, area, and line charts.
  • Each chart directly answers one analytical question, with tooltips and highlighting for clarity.
